Darwin Recommendations

Stokes Hill Wharf: buy fish & chips and a beer for $25 and watch the fish while you eat it, what more could you want – Michael

Coffee: Laneway Specialty Coffee, Sweet Brew & Co, and Ray’s café & patisserie are all good for a coffee and breakfast – Peta

Six Tanks Brew Pub: a microbrewery located conveniently on Mitchell Street – Andrew Wellington

Aquascene fish feeding: fun for the young and young at heart – Bev Stockwell

In Litchfield National Park: for a day trip from Darwin, depending on what is open after the wet, Florence Falls and Bluey Hole are great swimming spots, and if you don’t mind walking Green Ant Falls and Tjaynera Falls – Julia Harkin

For coffee in Litchfield, visit Kin Coffee near Wangi Falls on Marindja Road – Peta & Wello
